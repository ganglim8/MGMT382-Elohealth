The Elohealth Story

Ever since our first hospital opened in 1922, Elohealth has had one goal above all: providing the highest quality of healthcare possible to all members of our community. Elohealth has since opened more than 20 hospitals across the Pacific Northwest Area. Elohealth is proud to offer top-rate healthcare in all kinds of medical departments, with world-renowned doctors specializing in fields including:

  • Cardiology
  • Neurology
  • Oncology
  • Pediatric Services
  • Dermatology
