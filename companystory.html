The Elohealth Story

Ever since our first medical device launched in 1937, Elohealth has had one goal above all: the highest quality of medical-grade equipment to help service hospitals across the country. Elohealth has since sold thousands of medical equipment units to hospitals all over the United States, and has even aquired a medical clinic to better serve its community's medical needs. Elohealth is proud to offer top-rate care in all kinds of medical departments, with world-renowned doctors specializing in fields including:

  • Cardiology
  • Neurology
  • Oncology
  • Dermatology
  • Pediatric Services

Our founder, James Edward Scott, began this journey upon his graduation from Johns Hopkins University's medical program in 1921. While interning and completing his residency at a hospital in the area, he noticed the inefficiency amongst his fellow doctors due to the rudementary medical equipment they were provided with. Scott knew there was a better solution, and after years of testing and development alongside a team of medical scientists, Elohealth was born.

"When the opportunity to do good calls upon you, it is imperative you must answer" - James Edward Scott

Whether in the clinic providing top-rate healthcare to patients or in the R&D department innovating new ways to make care the best it can be, Elohealth abides by three core principles:

  1. Provide the highest quality possible
  2. Place the well-being of patients first
  3. Instill empathy and compassion in everything we do
